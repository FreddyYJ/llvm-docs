Detailed Description

This represents '#pragma omp parallel master taskloop simd' directive.

#pragma omp parallel master taskloop simd private(a,b) grainsize(val)
num_tasks(num)

In this example directive '#pragma omp parallel master taskloop simd' has clauses 'private' with the variables 'a' and 'b', 'grainsize' with expression 'val' and 'num_tasks' with expression 'num'.
